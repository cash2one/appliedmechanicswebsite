Welcome to Placement Office 2014-15

Deeds, not the words speak...

If you are looking for industry drivers, then your journey ends here. Trained in an excellent R&D oriented environment, our products have been shaping the industry over the last four decades. Involvement of our postgraduates in several real time research projects offered by govt. and private agencies speaks volumes of the credibility and quality of service the institute has to offer in the field of Applied Mechanics.

The Department of Applied Mechanics invites the Industry Leaders to participate in Placement 2014-15 to harness the potential of the chosen best minds of this country. Our students are groomed to the needs of the industry and look forward to fuel the industry in providing various engineering solutions, especially in the area of applied mechanics related to:

  • Solid Mechanics: Stress Analysis, FEA, mechanical testing, Impact mechanics, composites, smart structures
  • Fluid Mechanics: Pipeline engineering, slurry transportation, CFD, flow stability analysis and turbulence modeling, Testing and calibration, non-contact flow measurements.
  • Materials Engineering: fatigue and failure analysis, fracture mechanics, material testing
  • Design Engineering: CAD/CAE, Design optimization, design sensitivity analysis.

The department looks forward to a long term happy partnership with you. For more details download the brochure
